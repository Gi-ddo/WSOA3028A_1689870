The importance of Accessibility

Accessibility in web development is the practice of making websites usable by as many people as possible. They can be generally viewed as making your website accessible by the disabled, users on mobile devices and people with poor network connections.

This practice is important because first of all by making your website accessible you will bring more people into your website to use it and this is important because if developers were making a service based website therefore many people would be able to use it because its user friendly for everyone and benefits the developers as well in them being successful in their businesses.

The practice is also important because it shows good moral and ethical values. Making a website accessible is indeed important but how to make it accessible is an important skill to have. The ways developers make their websites accessible are as follows:

  1. Semantic markup: With the correct use of semantic markup we make our websites accessible for users and it makes our websites easier to find on the web.

  2. Extensions: These are key because they provide more options for people. These include software to help with audio impairment, visual impairment tools like zooming features, keyboard functionality for people with physical disability.
