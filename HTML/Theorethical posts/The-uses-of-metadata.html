The uses of metadata

Metadata is defined as the data that describes data. what does that mean? It essentially means that we are given more informatio to describe the data we are presenting in our documents. In web development metadata is extreamly important because it gives life and more meanining to our websites. Examples of meta data include the title of our website, our websites would not any value if they did not a title. Search engines would not be able to find our websies and we do not want this. Another example is website descriptions, website descriptions give an overview of what to expect when someone decides to click on your website. This why most web developers try their best to make their website descriptions as appealing as possible.

A really trivial example would be from the social media websites as soon as we search for Facebook or Instagram we get this very complelling descriptions telling us to sign up and we can share and post all sorts of things that we can share with friends and family. Without meta descriptions most websites would not be visitied because people do not want to waste their time looking at sites that do not draw them into clicking on it. This use of metadata also links very well with accessibility, to give context the charset metadata module makes the websites render every single Character set known to man. This means that you can make your site accessible to eveyone from every country because your website can translate into their language. This use of metadata is really powerful and should be inforced more into web development.
