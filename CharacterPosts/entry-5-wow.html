WOW!


Date: 1 January 2000


Ok so now here we are, I am intrigued about the device the manager was using butI can't simply ask him to tell me what the device is without blowing my cover, I think it is stime to show my worth. During our training sessions back home we had to show exceptional levels of awareness and situational adaptation, basically it means how well someone can adapt to a situation to their advantage, so because of the AI technology salvaged through ruins the organization was able to create simulations for us to exercise our skills and I was exceptional at them which is one of the contributing factors for being picked for this mission. Now I could use this opportunity to ask the boy for some information while he is viewing the computer, children are usually very ignorant about a lot of things so he is the best person to ask as he will not get too suspicious and ask a lot of questions. The mother takes her son to the computer booth and leaves to have a look at the other items in the store. I approach the boy with the utmost confidence, I first start talking to him about the computer and getting his thoughts on it, he likes it!. Now the boy is very sharp and has a good understanding for technology which shooked me for a boy that was 12 years old as he mentioned, so I begin to ask him about the strange device and he tells that the device is what they call the telephone, a device used to communicate to people from all over the world, my first reaction was WOW.
